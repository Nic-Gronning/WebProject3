Welcome to Bellevue, Washington

Discover the beauty, attractions, and unique offerings of Bellevue - a favorite place for visitors.

Why Visit Bellevue?

Bellevue is known for its stunning parks, vibrant shopping districts, and a thriving cultural scene. Located near Seattle, it offers a blend of urban and natural experiences that make it an ideal destination for all types of travelers.

  • Population: Approx. 150,000
  • Weather: Mild, with rainy winters and warm summers
  • Languages: Primarily English, with a diverse population

Downtown Park

This 21-acre park in downtown Bellevue features a circular promenade, a large open lawn, and a beautiful waterfall. A perfect place to relax and take in the city views.

Meydenbauer Bay Park

Located on the waterfront, Meydenbauer Bay Park offers scenic views, beach access, and plenty of family-friendly activities.

Bellevue Arts Museum

Bellevue Arts Museum (BAM) showcases contemporary visual art, craft, and design. It features rotating exhibitions and offers workshops, making it a must-visit for art enthusiasts.
